Quang-Binh Nguyen

I am a Research Resident at Qualcomm AI Research, advised by Staff Scientist Dr. Khoi Nguyen. Previously, I obtained my Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science - Honors Program at University of Science, VNU-HCM, under the supervision of Prof. Minh-Triet Tran, and Dr. Trung-Nghia Le.

My research focuses on controllable image generation, particulary image personalization (diffusion and autoregressive models). Recenty, I have been working on test-time alignment for generative models.

I'd love to connect with people who share similar interests! Feel free to reach out via email!
